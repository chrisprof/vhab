Statistics

Yearly drug abuse causes over 70,000 deaths in the USA alone.

This number increases annually by 4.0%

The overall life expectancy of people in the US had a dip between 2014 and 2019 because of drug abuse.

An estimated 60% of bad work performance is caused by drugs

Approx. 50% of vehicular accidents are caused by drugs

Approx. 50% of deaths by suicide are also caused by drugs

data was sourced by SAMHSA
