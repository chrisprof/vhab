[ Welcome to vHab ]

vHab is a free online tool for struggling drug addicts looking to change their life. The easy-to-use daily dose reducer and clean counter helps addicts manage their lives without entering a state of withdrawal. Users can manage their addiction by following the daily dose reducer. The progress made will be shown neatly through means of a graph. The Clean Counter gives survivors a feeling of accomplishment as they look back at all they have done. vHab wasn't just made for drug addicts; but also concerned mothers, fathers, sons, daughters, and others. The family help center provides detailed instructions regarding how to help a loved one from addiction. The drug database to teaches all about specific drugs and understand how to counteract them. Lastly, vHab provides General info, including links/numbers to certified professionals if needed.

What vHab offers:

01

The Drug Database

The Drug Database is database built into vHab which gives information on a certain type of drug. One can reference the database if they need more information on the health effects of the drug that they are taking, or they want to know more about. This data will help to inform people and families of the benefits and dangers of doing a certain drug.

02

The Daily Dose Reducer

The Daily Dose Reducer is represented through a chart that shows the progress of one's dose reduction. One just has to give basic information, such as, name, drug being taken, and the daily dose taken.

03

The Clean Counter

The Clean Counter shows how long you have been on your sobriety journey. This provides a dopamine reward whenever a new milestone has been reached. The dopamine reward is a harmless and beneficial substitute for the drug.
