[ Welcome to vHab ]

vHab is a free online tool for struggling drug addicts looking to change their life. The easy-to-use daily dose reducer and clean counter helps addicts manage their lives without entering a state of withdrawal. Users can manage their addiction by following the daily dose reducer. The progress made will be shown neatly through means of a graph. The Clean Counter gives survivors a feeling of accomplishment as they look back at all they have done. vHab wasn't just made for drug addicts; but also concerned mothers, fathers, sons, daughters, and others. The family help center provides detailed instructions regarding how to help a loved one from addiction. The drug database to teaches all about specific drugs and understand how to counteract them. Lastly, vHab provides General info, including links/numbers to certified professionals if needed.

What vHab offers:

01

The Drug Database

The Drug Database (TDD) is one of the more informational components of vHab. Unlike The Daily Dose Reducer or The Clean Counter, TDD is meant for concerned family members and the general public to read. It gives readers a deeper understanding of what drugs their family members might have taken and how to help them.

02

The Daily Dose Reducer

The Daily Dose Reducer is a vital resource for addicts looking to slowly manage and eliminate drugs from their system without entering a state of withdrawal. Users simply have to start their journey by scrolling down and entering their basic information (ie. Name, drug name, and amount of drug taken daily.) before being re-directed to the user profile page. Here vHab decreses the dosage of the user's current drug daily slowly but suely. vHab does this in order to eradicate any chances of withdrawl systoms rising within users.

03

The Family Help Page

The family help page (FHP) is a public resource for family members looking to help a loved one out of addiction. It details the best way to help a loved one with an addiction and keep the confusing/scary parts at bay. The FHP was cultivated for concred mothers, fathers, sons, daughers, wives, husbandsands, and other family memebrs.
